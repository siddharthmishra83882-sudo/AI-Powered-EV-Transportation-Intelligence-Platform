# ⚡ AI-Powered EV Transport  Intelligence Platform

## Overview

The AI-Powered EV Transport Intelligence Platform is a data-driven analytics and forecasting system designed to analyze the rapidly growing Electric Vehicle (EV) market. The platform combines business intelligence, interactive visualizations, and machine learning-driven insights to help stakeholders understand market trends, vehicle performance, and demand patterns.

This project was developed as a transportation analytics and market intelligence solution, demonstrating how data science and AI can support decision-making in the electric mobility ecosystem.

---

## Problem Statement

The electric vehicle industry is experiencing unprecedented growth. Manufacturers, investors, policymakers, and consumers require intelligent tools to analyze:

* Market demand
* Vehicle performance
* Battery technologies
* Manufacturer competitiveness
* Consumer adoption trends

Traditional reporting systems often fail to provide real-time interactive insights and predictive capabilities.

---

## Proposed Solution

This platform provides:

* Interactive EV market analytics dashboard
* Vehicle performance analysis
* Battery technology insights
* Manufacturer-wise market comparison
* Country-wise manufacturing trends
* AI-powered demand forecasting framework
* Business intelligence visualizations

The system enables users to transform raw EV data into actionable market intelligence.
